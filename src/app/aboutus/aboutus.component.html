About Us

For me the foray into making chocolates after having spent working over a decade in corporate life happened by “chance and luck” showered on me after the birth of my daughter. I decided to give up full time 9 to 6 job to pursue chocolate making as more than a mere kitchen hobby and that’s how the idea of Reema Singh’s The Chocolate Factory was born. So it’s as old as my elder daughter now
It has been an amazing journey so far and I wish to keep doing what I love the most now after my family: chocolate making
